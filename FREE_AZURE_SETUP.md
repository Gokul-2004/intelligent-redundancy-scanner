# Free Azure AD Setup - M365 Developer Program

## Problem
You can't register an app without an Azure AD directory/tenant.

## Solution: Join M365 Developer Program (FREE)

### Step 1: Join Developer Program
1. Go to https://developer.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/dev-program
2. Click "Join now" or "Get started"
3. Sign in with your Microsoft account
4. Fill out the form (name, country, etc.)
5. Accept terms and join

### Step 2: Get Your Developer Tenant
1. After joining, you'll get access to a **free developer tenant**
2. This gives you:
   - Free Azure AD tenant
   - Ability to register apps
   - Free M365 E5 developer subscription (for testing)

### Step 3: Access Azure Portal
1. Go to https://portal.azure.com
2. Sign in with the account you used for the developer program
3. You should now have access to Azure Active Directory

### Step 4: Register Your App
Now you can follow the normal setup:
1. Go to Azure Portal → Azure Active Directory → App registrations
2. Click "New registration"
3. Register your app as usual

## Alternative: Sign Up for Azure (Free Tier)

If you prefer:
1. Go to https://azure.microsoft.com/free/
2. Sign up for free Azure account
3. You get $200 free credit + free tier services
4. This also gives you an Azure AD tenant

## Which Should You Choose?

**M365 Developer Program** (Recommended):
- ✅ Completely free
- ✅ Quick setup (5 minutes)
- ✅ Perfect for development
- ✅ Includes M365 E5 for testing

**Azure Free Tier**:
- ✅ $200 free credit
- ✅ More services available
- ⚠️ Requires credit card (but won't charge if you stay in free tier)

**Recommendation**: Use M365 Developer Program - it's faster and perfect for this use case!
